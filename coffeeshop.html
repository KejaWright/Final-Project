Game Rules & Instructions

These are the basic rules of the game! Follow them carefully!

  1. This game requires you to be signed in to play to record scores.
  2. There are 5 levels to this game, with each harder than the last.
  3. You will have a time limit of 2 minutes, which decreases by 20 seconds every level.
  4. The complexity of the drink increases with level as well.
  5. The goal is to make as many drinks (with ingredients in the correct order) as you can within the time limit.
  6. For every correct cup made, you gain 50 points. Incorrectly subitted cups are -20 points.
  7. The color of the "coffee cup" gives you a hint as to what type of blend it is asking for
  8. You can reset your drink if you mess up.
  9. When the level is finished, you can either retry the level, restart the game, or move on to the next.
  10. When all levels are finished, total score will be determined, and rank given based on score.
  11. Your score along with your username will be posted on the leaderboard.
  12. Only top 5 will be featured on the home page.
  13. Background music: Mii Channel - Mii Plaza (Lofi Lia Remix)
